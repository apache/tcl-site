Contact information for mod_dtcl

All enquiries regarding mod_dtcl (both usage and development) should be directed towards the mailing list at mod_dtcl@tcl.apache.org. It is not necessary to be subscribed to the mailing list to post, although you might want to set a reply-to.

To subscribe to the mailing list, send an empty email to mod_dtcl-subscribe@tcl.apache.org.

The list archives can be found (for now) at marc.theaimsgroup.com.
