Contact

To contact the Apache Rivet team, please write to our mailing list, at rivet-dev@tcl.apache.org .

This list is archived here: http://mail-archives.apache.org/mod_mbox/tcl-rivet-dev/

To subscribe, send email to rivet-dev-subscribe@tcl.apache.org

Credits

Apache Rivet owes its existance to a lot of people. First and foremost, to the people behind the Apache Web Server and the Tool Command Language .

Rivet's authors are Damon Courtney , David Welton and Karl Lehenbauer .

Massimo Manghi is taking care of the project doing new developments and fixing bugs. He's also packaging Rivet for Debian/Ubuntu.

The nice logo was created by Randy Stanard, of DeWitt Designs .
